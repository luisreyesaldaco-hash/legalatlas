The architecture
01

Query expansion

Magnus generates 5 jurisdiction-specific search queries per country, translating legal concepts to local terminology. "Wrongful termination" becomes "skončení pracovního poměru" in Czech, "despido improcedente" in Spanish.

02

Parallel semantic search

Searches 153,105 articles across all selected jurisdictions in parallel using vector embeddings. Results are ranked by semantic relevance, not just keyword match.

03

Cross-jurisdictional synthesis

Claude Sonnet analyzes conflicts, identifies governing law (Rome I, Brussels I bis), and gives concrete recommendations — with every claim backed by a verified article.
