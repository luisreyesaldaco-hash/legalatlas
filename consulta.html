APOLO — LEGAL QUERY
My landlord wants to enter my apartment without permission
A
⚖️ Art. 2412 · Código Civil Federal

"El arrendador no podrá entrar al inmueble arrendado sin autorización expresa del arrendatario, salvo causa de urgencia debidamente justificada."

Your landlord does not have the right to enter your apartment without your explicit consent. Access is only permitted in emergencies that are duly justified. You may deny entry and document any unauthorized attempts.
