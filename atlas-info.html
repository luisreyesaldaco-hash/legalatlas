The concept
Thesis

Laws that support you

Laws that support your legal position. Planets in prograde orbit — moving with you. The stronger the legal basis, the closer the planet to the center of the universe.

Antithesis

Laws that oppose you

Laws that oppose your position or create legal obstacles. Planets in retrograde orbit — moving against you. Distance from the center represents legal risk and weight.

How Atlas works

STEP 01

⚖️

Describe the dialectic

Enter your thesis (your legal position) and antithesis (the opposing position). Atlas analyzes both simultaneously across all selected jurisdictions.

STEP 02

🔍

Semantic search across jurisdictions

Finds supporting and opposing articles in all selected countries simultaneously using vector embeddings. Every article is ranked by directional relevance.

STEP 03

🪐

3D visualization

Laws become planets. Click any planet to see all articles from that statute and their relevance score. Explore the legal universe of your case.
