What is the deadline to file after wrongful termination?
APOLO · Under § 72 of the Zákoník práce, the employee has 2 months from the date on which the employment relationship was supposed to end to file a lawsuit challenging the termination. This deadline is absolute — missing it extinguishes the right to contest. The action must be filed with the competent district court (okresní soud) at the employer's registered address.
