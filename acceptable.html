Acceptable Use Policy

Effective as of April 7, 2026

Tesseum is a legal research tool designed for legal professionals, students, and citizens seeking verified legal information. This policy establishes the permitted and prohibited uses of the platform.

1. PERMITTED USES

Tesseum may be used for:

  • Academic and professional legal research
  • Consulting current legislation in available jurisdictions
  • Preliminary contract analysis to identify legal risks
  • Legal education and training
  • Case preparation as an auxiliary tool
  • Comparison of regulatory frameworks across jurisdictions

2. PROHIBITED USES

Violation of these prohibitions may result in immediate account suspension without prior notice.

The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Attempting to manipulate the Apolo engine through prompt injection or jailbreak techniques
  • Requesting information to facilitate illegal activities
  • Uploading documents containing third-party personal data without their consent
  • Using the service to harass, threaten, or harm others
  • Performing automated scraping or denial-of-service attacks
  • Reproducing or redistributing generated content for commercial purposes without authorization
  • Impersonating another user or Tesseum
  • Attempting to access other users' data or internal systems
  • Using the service to evade one's own legal obligations

3. TOOL LIMITATIONS

Users acknowledge that:

  • Tesseum is an auxiliary tool, not a substitute for professional legal advice
  • Apolo's analyses are advisory and must be verified by a qualified attorney before making legal decisions
  • Legislation changes — the corpus is updated periodically but may not reflect recent amendments
  • Results may contain errors and must be cross-checked against official sources

4. SYSTEM INTEGRITY

Tesseum implements active protections against manipulation of the Apolo engine. Attempts to circumvent these protections are logged and may result in permanent account suspension and, in serious cases, legal action under applicable law.

5. REPORTING ABUSE

If you detect misuse of the platform or a security vulnerability, report it to hola@tesseum.com. Good-faith reports will be treated with confidentiality.

6. CONSEQUENCES OF VIOLATION

Violations of this policy may result in:

  • Temporary or permanent account suspension
  • Subscription cancellation without refund
  • Reporting to competent authorities in cases of illegal activity
  • Legal action for damages caused to Tesseum or third parties

7. CONTACT

For questions about this policy: hola@tesseum.com
